Interpretation of Gleeman

GLEEMAN: GLEE (Joy) + MAN (Human; man), A Gleeman is a talented and brilliant entertainer, bringing FUN, FASHION & CREATIVENESS thus to re-shape lifestyle with our outstanding products.

SGLEEMAN has been consistently striving to provide innovative and quality product to customers worldwide aiming to bring them incredible audio performance.
Shenzhen Gleeman Technology Co., Ltd, established in 2015, is a subsidiary of Hong Kong Gleeman Enterprises Company Limited who has been operated since 2010. With our seasoned professional team of ID Design, Acoustics Design, PCB Layout and RF Design, we have strong faith on our dedication to provide superb audio products worldwide, bringing extraordi-nary sound performance to our valuable customers.
