BeoLink Multiroom Multiroom music
Bang & Olufsen (B&O) is wedded to the idea that technology should make life better and simpler. The Multiroom multi-room music series that connects B&O classic design and retains perfect sound quality is the embodiment of this concept, aiming to let you indulge in the charm and beauty of music at any time, and let the sounds of nature flow freely in every corner of your home.

The Multiroom multi-room music series is plugged into a coherent, easy-to-use wireless system that can be controlled via a mobile device or by tapping on a speaker. As you walk around the house, just touch one of the multi-room music series products, and they instantly connect seamlessly to the rest of the system. You can choose to play different music in different rooms, or you can play the same melody in all rooms. You can also use popular technologies (like built-in Chromecast, Apple AirPlay, or bluetooth) to play directly from your favorite music application, or use the new B&O app to enjoy a full range of B&O premium music experiences.
