Gleeman

Shenzhen gleeman technology company is a jointly enterprise founded by R&D and producer team. It has experienced engineers and strong manufacturing platform. As a high-tech enterprise, gleeman technology specialized in design and produce wireless earphones, Bluetooth speakers and multimedia audio products, it have independent R&D,Produce and sales department, can meet the needs of different customers.

Since its establishment in 2010, gleeman technology has trained a professional R&D teams, included ID design, acoustic design, layout and RF design. Now the factory in Dongguan has 6 Assembling lines, 200 workers and 30 QC staffs, monthly production capacity over 300,000 units. Gleeman technology is aimed to offer customers Innovative Product with good quality at a competitive price.

The company's brand “Gleeman” is dedicated to providing the best digital audio products for users worldwide, bringing users pleasant product experience
